Family

My family

My name is Aydinbek Musabek. My life began in Turkestan, but now I live in Almaty. Turkestan is my home, full of warm memories, a place where I witnessed my childhood. My family is my biggest supporters. My mother, grandmother and grandfather live in Turkestan, and although I am in Almaty, they are always close to me. Their trust and support in me drives me forward, helps me smile in difficult moments and strive for new heights. For me, being an only child is a responsibility and obligation. My main goal is to live up to the expectations of my mother and grandfather.
